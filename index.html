How Scores Are Calculated

The percentages you see are part of a driver incentive program. Hereâ€™s a simple breakdown of how the "Final %" is calculated:

1. Base Rate

Every driver starts with a fixed "Base Rate" percentage. This is the foundation of their pay percentage for the week.

2. Bonuses & Penalties

Various performance metrics are measured each week. These metrics add or subtract percentage points from the Base Rate.

3. Final Percentage

The "Final %" column is the result of all these numbers combined. The formula is:

[Base Rate] + [All Bonuses %] - [All Penalties %] = Final %
